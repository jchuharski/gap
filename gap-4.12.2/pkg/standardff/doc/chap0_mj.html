WARNING: This package implements the constructions in the paper [Lüb22]. As long as that paper is not published we reserve the right to still change the definition of standard polynomials defining finite fields and standard generators of cyclic multiplicative subgroups.

In version 1.0 and later the definitions will not be changed anymore.
